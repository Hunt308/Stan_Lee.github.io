Stan Lee
Stan Lee (1928-2018) was the owner of the Marvel Comics brand best knwn for his (with help of Steve Ditko and Jack Kirby) create iconic characters like Spider-Man, X-Men, The Avengers, Fanastic Four, and many more. He is also best known for making cameos in almost every on-screen content from the Marvel Comics brand such as Movies, TV Shows, and even Video Games as well. He is arguably the last member of the Golden Age of Comic Books era to live for that long.
